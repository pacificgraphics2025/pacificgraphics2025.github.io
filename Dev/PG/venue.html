How to Reach the Venue

By MRT

Take the Red Line to Dongmen Station (東門).
-> Exit at Exit 3.
-> After exiting, walk straight ahead.
-> Keep walking straight for about 5–10 minutes, passing a few small alleys.
-> Turn right when you see a small lane (you may see signs for "Jinhua Street").
-> Then turn left and continue straight until you reach the venue.
This walk takes approximately 8 minutes.
