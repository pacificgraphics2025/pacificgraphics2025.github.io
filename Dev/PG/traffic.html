Traffic Information


Airport to Taipei City

We provide transportation options from TPE and TSA to Taipei Main Station, the central hub of Taipei's transit system. From there, our conference venue is only a 16-minute ride by MRT.


Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport (TPE) to Taipei Main Station

By MRT (Recommended) Each terminal of TPE has a stop ( Airport Terminal 1 Station or Airport Terminal 2 Station ).
Both   Commuter Train   and   Express Train   can arrive at Taipei Main Station.
The express service taking only 35 minutes from Taoyuan Airport Terminal 1 to Taipei Main Station and 38 minutes from Terminal 2 to Taipei Main Station. Even the commuter service that stops at all stations takes only 50 minutes.
The fare of both trains is roughly TWD$150.
The Taoyuan Airport MRT map is available here.
By Taxi Taxi services are available at Terminal 1 ( 1st floor, East Gate 12 ) and Terminal 2 ( 1st floor, East Gate 26 ) in the arrival halls.
The taxi fare to Taipei Main Station area is roughly TWD$1,000 ~ TWD$1,500.

Taipei International Airport (TSA) to Taipei Main Station

By MRT (Recommended) The MRT ride takes approximately 16 minutes and costs roughly TWD$25.
The Taipei Metro Route Map is available here.
  1. TSA has a stop, "Songshan Airport Station", on the   Brown Line  . Please catch the train at the platform heading toward "Taipei Zoo" direction.
  2. Change train at "Zhongxiao Fuxing Station" and catch the train at the   Blue Line   platform heading towards "Taipei Main Station" direction.
  3. Please get off at "Taipei Main Station".
By Taxi Taxi services are available outside the arrivals lobby and in front of Column 13 - 14.
The taxi fare to Taipei Main Station area is roughly TWD$250.
